These are your stats!

Adaptability is your ability to make use of your environment including attacks and reflect it back to the attacker. This trait belongs to the Water Village. 

Resilience is your ability to withstand attacks and external forces. This trait belongs to the Earth Nation. 

Aggression is your ability to harness your inner light and consistently exert it on external beings. This trait belongs to the Fire Kingdom.

Agility is your ability to dodge and evade any targeted attacks in an attempt to tire out the attacker. This trait belongs to the Wind Temple. 

Eligibility determines your ability to move on to the final battle in the underworld.
